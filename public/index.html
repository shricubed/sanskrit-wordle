How to Play

Guess the hidden Sanskrit word in 6 tries. Type 5 akshara (syllables) into the input box, then press Enter.

Syllables = consonant + vowel

Type a consonant then a vowel — they merge into one akshara live in the box:

m → म (pending)  →  m+a → म  |  m+aa → मा
n+ii → नी  |  r+u → रु  |  h+aa → हा

The gold text in the box is the syllable still being formed.
